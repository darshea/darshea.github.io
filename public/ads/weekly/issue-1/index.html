Scoring how AI will disrupt public companies — before the market prices it in.

Issue #1 · March 14, 2026

The AI Displacement Score (ADS) measures structural risk from AI disruption across five dimensions: labor vulnerability, revenue durability, pricing power, intermediation thickness, and adaptation speed. Scale: 0–100. Higher = greater displacement risk.

This week: 10 companies scored. 5 most at risk. 5 best positioned.
