ADS Weekly · Issue #8

The AI Restructuring Wave

May 6, 2026 · 10 companies scored · Three CEOs disclosed AI displacement in one week — Freshworks, Coinbase, and DXC confirm what TaskUs already knew

Issue #8 arrives in the middle of the most concentrated week of AI displacement disclosures in 2026. On May 5 alone: Freshworks cut 11% of its workforce (CEO: "over half our code is written by AI"), Coinbase cut 14% ("engineers ship in days what used to take teams weeks"), and DXC Technology hit a historic stock low while announcing AI deployed for all 115,000 employees. We scored the full displacement spectrum this week — from TaskUs (74, HIGH), the most directly exposed BPO company in our universe, to ASML (26, LOW), the EUV monopoly that is a structural AI beneficiary. CrowdStrike (36) scores LOW because the same AI cycle driving BPO displacement is expanding its addressable threat landscape.
