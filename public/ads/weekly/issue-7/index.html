The AI Efficiency Dividend

April 29, 2026 · 10 companies scored · Restructuring, repositioning, and the new beneficiaries — who captures the efficiency dividend?

This week spans the full displacement range: Kforce (72) leads the HIGH tier — IT staffing faces simultaneous AI attack on both the labor it places and the recruiters who place it. AMD (22) and Cloudflare (26) score near the ADS floor — semiconductor and network infrastructure that AI runs on, not through. The week's central insight: AI is restructuring workforces and compressing intermediaries, while the physical and network layers compound. NICE (38) is the counterintuitive case — a contact center software company capturing the AI spending that is eliminating the BPO industry.
