Methodology

The AI Displacement Score (ADS) is a proprietary analytical framework measuring structural exposure to AI-driven disruption. Each company is scored 0–100 across five independent dimensions, weighted equally.

D1 — Labor VulnerabilityCore workforce substitutability by current AI
D2 — Revenue DurabilityRevenue stream defensibility under AI competition
D3 — Competitive MoatPricing power & switching costs vs. AI-native alternatives
D4 — Intermediation RiskAI bypass potential of core value-chain position
D5 — Adaptation CapacityStrategic & product response to the AI transition
