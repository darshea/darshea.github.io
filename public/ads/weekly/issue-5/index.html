The AI Economy's Winners and Losers

April 15, 2026 · 10 companies scored · From BPO disruption to quantum opportunity

This week spans the full spectrum of AI displacement. Conduent (70) and ZipRecruiter (70) join the HIGH risk tier — BPO processing and job boards face the same structural headwinds as staffing firms. IonQ (38) and Vistra (34) score LOW — quantum computing infrastructure and power generation are among the most AI-resistant business models in the public market. Six companies land in MEDIUM, including Uber's autonomous vehicle reckoning and The Trade Desk's post-earnings-miss AI pivot test.
