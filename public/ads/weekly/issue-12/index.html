Methodology

The AI Displacement Score (ADS) quantifies structural exposure to AI-driven disruption for publicly traded equities. Each company is evaluated on five independent dimensions, equally weighted into a composite 0–100 score.

D1 — Labor VulnerabilityDegree to which core workforce is substitutable by AI.
D2 — Revenue DurabilityStability of revenue under AI competitive pressure.
D3 — Competitive MoatPricing power, switching costs, and network effects vs. AI-native alternatives.
D4 — Intermediation RiskExtent to which AI can bypass the company's value-chain position.
D5 — Adaptation CapacityPace and scale of AI strategy execution.
