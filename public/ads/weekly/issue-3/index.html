ADS scores companies 0–100 across five dimensions: Labor Vulnerability, Revenue Durability, Pricing Power, Intermediation, and Adaptation Speed. Higher score = more AI displacement risk. This issue brings the cumulative universe to 30 companies across Issues #1–3.
