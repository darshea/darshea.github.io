The AI Economy's Infrastructure Layer

April 22, 2026 · 10 companies scored · From nuclear power to voice AI — who builds the atoms AI runs on, and who gets displaced

This week spans the full displacement spectrum. TTEC Holdings (68) and LivePerson (68) lead the HIGH tier — CX outsourcing and conversational AI platforms face terminal disruption dynamics. Taiwan Semiconductor (26) and Constellation Energy (28) score near the ADS floor — physical infrastructure that AI runs on, not through. The week's central irony: LivePerson, a company that sells conversational AI, is being disrupted by conversational AI — one of the clearest self-referential displacement cases the framework has scored.
